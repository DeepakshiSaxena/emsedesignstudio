About ESME Design Studio

Bespoke Residential Design

Your home should be a reflection of your journey, your tastes, and your aspirations. We specialize in creating one-of-a-kind residences that balance bold architectural statements with the intimate comforts of home.

A detail-oriented architect with a passion for clean lines and functional spaces, blending creativity with precision. From site selection to the final fixture, we guide you through a collaborative journey to translate your vision into a sanctuary that is uniquely yours.

Value Delivered: A well-designed home can reduce energy costs by 30%, increase resale value by 15-20%, and save you months of stress during the construction process.
